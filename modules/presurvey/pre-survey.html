Westside Bike Share Pre-Survey

Thank you for participating in the Westside Bike Share program!

The Transportation Research and Education Center at Portland State University is conducting the evaluation of the users of the program. The first survey will focus on your current travel behavior and demographics. A second survey will come in the summer and will ask questions about your experiences using the program.

To participate in the brief survey please follow this survey link: https://portlandstate.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_8rkHCakPtRnopOR

Thanks for your participation in the program.
